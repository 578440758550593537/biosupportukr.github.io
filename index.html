"Support Ukrainians" campaign is a
joint initiative of the Biosphere
Corporation (the biggest
Ukrainian manufacturer and
distributor of household and
hygiene products) with top
European retailers to support
Ukrainian producers.

The campaign will be supported
by the Embassies of Ukraine.

The goal of the project is to
support the Ukrainian
producers and economy in these challenging times.
Biosphere Corporation is one of
the largest Ukrainian
manufacturers and exporters of
household and personal care
products in Ukraine.
The corporation has three own
production facilities, a logistics
complex, and branches in
different parts of Ukraine.
Biosphere Corporation employs
more than 2,000 people.

The corporation produces
essential goods in such a difficult
time for Ukrainians and does
everything possible to ensure
that all of them have the goods
they need.

These are diapers for children
who are forced to hide in
basements and subway stations.
Wet wipes that are so needed for
the defenders of our country,
who are on the battlefield or
patrolling the cities. Garbage
bags which are used daily by
rescue teams and volunteers to
collect the remains of buildings
that were destroyed by shells.

Together we will overcome all the hurdles
and most importantly - win!

Thank you for your contribution and support of Ukraine and every Ukrainian!
